  • VPN

    Our VPN services can be used for voice transmissions. It is possible to have multiple IP's with one account, but a special VPN configuration will be needed. We will provide you with a configuration which allows the VoIP gateway and VPN server to act as one LAN. All voice traffic will be bypassed through a VPN tunnel between your location and our VPN server, and you will be able toassign IP's to each VoIP gateway. This setup is compatible with GNU/Linux OS only, so it needs to be installed on the VPN gateways, both client and server.
